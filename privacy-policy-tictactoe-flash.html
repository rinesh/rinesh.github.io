Privacy Policy for Tic-Tac-Toe Flash

Last updated: June 6, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use Tic-Tac-Toe Flash, a classic strategy game mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

1. About This App

Tic-Tac-Toe Flash is a fast-paced, modern take on the classic tic-tac-toe game. The app features single-player mode against AI, local multiplayer, and various difficulty levels and game modes to enhance the traditional 3x3 grid experience.

3. Information We Collect

Tic-Tac-Toe Flash is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Game statistics or scores
  • Contact information
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Network activity or browsing history
  • Any other personal or sensitive user data

How the App Works:

  • All game logic and AI calculations are performed locally on your device
  • Game preferences and settings are stored locally on your device only
  • Local scores and statistics (if any) remain on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No internet connection is required for the game to function
